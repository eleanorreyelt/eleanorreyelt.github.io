About

Eleanor Reyelt (b. Boston, 2004) is a visual artist based in Boston, Massachusetts. She works primarily in painting and textiles to create bold, material-driven works that celebrate the loud, electric energy of girlhood. Drawing from early aesthetics like glitter, plastic, and pink, her practice reimagines these elements with confidence and intensity. Through saturated surfaces, layered textures, and playful materials, she explores excess, visibility, and the power of being too much. Eleanor is currently pursuing her BFA in Painting with a minor in Fibers at the Savannah College of Art and Design.
